<div class="bio-intro">
I am a researcher at <strong>Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)</strong>, working at the intersection of <strong>Robotics</strong>, <strong>Reinforcement Learning</strong>, and <strong>Task Planning</strong>. My research focuses on enabling autonomous agents to learn from imperfect demonstrations and generalize to long-horizon, open-world tasks.

I am particularly interested in how diffusion models and imitation learning can power scalable robot planning.
</div>
